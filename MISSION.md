# Mission: LLM Fine-Tuning and Transfer Learning for Medical & Intelligent Applications

## Why
Enable the creation of intelligent, specialized applications on consumer GPU hardware by mastering LLM fine-tuning and transfer learning, moving from raw medical text and imagery to structured, validated clinical outputs.

## Success looks like
- Build an application to structure clinical text from open-ended medical anamnesis.
- Build an application to classify document types from image PDFs.
- Build an application to segment bone scintigraphy images and automatically generate a pre-medical report draft for physician validation.

## Constraints
- **Hardware**: Ubuntu Linux, RTX 4060 Ti (16 GB VRAM), 32 GB RAM, Ryzen 7 5800X3D.
- **Terminal**: zsh.
- **Learning Style**: Balanced mix of theory and hands-on practice, centering on step-by-step tutorial applications.
- **Data**: Rely strictly on open/publicly accessible datasets (with clear instructions on how to acquire them).

## Out of scope
- Training models from scratch.
- Production deployment infrastructure (deferred to a later stage of learning).
